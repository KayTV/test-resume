Projects

Galvanize Blog

Technical blog written about going through the Galvanize Full Stack Program, written in MEAN stack.


Movie Mania

Website created to keep track of movies you rated and want to see. Modified throughout the entire Galvanize Program. Previously written in Vanilla JavaScript, currently written in AngularJS with PostgreSQL and OMDB API.

Multiplayer game(up to 4 players) that mocks Nintendo's Super Smash Brothers only with your phone as your game controller. Written in Canvas, Express, JavaScript, and Sockets.
